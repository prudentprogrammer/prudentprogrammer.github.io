local_cafePersonal Life

Outside of my professional life, I involve myself in activities which involve minimal technology, maximum satisfaction, and are most meaningful. I spend my recreational time in three areas:

Music

I have a tremendous interest in music and play the Bamboo Flute (a.k.a the Bansuri). I have been playing this instrument since 3 years. Starting a few years ago, a small interest in music sparked into something bigger and meaningful. The events which I have performed so far are: Davis International Festival 2014, ASUCD Open Mic, Davis Music Talent Show, I-House Open Mic, and Sophia's Kitchen Open Mic.

Knowledge

I have recently (summer 2015) developed the habit of reading a few pages of a book every week instead of squandering time on Facebook. I mainly love reading autobiographies of amazing people and other inspirational books. Doing so has benefited me in many ways and have provided me with an opportunity to learn and think about spending life in meaningful ways. Occasionally though, you might catch me reading horror books, especially those of Stephen King.

Meditation

I have been practicing pranayaam (the art of breathing / breathing exercises) since the summer of 2014. These actions have been so effective that I have rarely fallen sick and have NEVER taken medicines for sickness since 2014 (true story!!). Life is truly amazing since then in many ways and yoga has definitely enhanced my physical, emotional, spiritual, and mental abilities.
